Though we may get frustrated with others, or even ourselves, we must take a step back for a clearer understanding. Misunderstandings are the root of all our troubles. You can debate me on this one, but I wholeheartedly believe it to be true. Focusing on every perspective is a key skill I have learned, not only while working, but while living.
